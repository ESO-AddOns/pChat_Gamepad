when you use [URL="https://www.esoui.com/downloads/info93-pChatChatcustomizationhelplooknotificationsoundautomationhistory.html"]pChat[/URL] and turn [QUOTE]OPTIONS > GAMEPLAY > USE KEYBOARD CHAT[/QUOTE] off.
